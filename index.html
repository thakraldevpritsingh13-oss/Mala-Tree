Our Story

Rooted in tradition, strung by hand

Mala Tree began as a simple idea: spiritual jewelry should feel personal, not mass-produced. Founded by Simerbersingh Bedi in Patiala, every piece that leaves our hands — a single rudraksha bracelet or a full 108-bead mala — is selected, cleaned, and strung individually, the same way it has been done for generations.

Our Mission

To make genuine, energetically sound spiritual products accessible to practitioners everywhere, without compromising on authenticity.

Quality Promise

Every rudraksha is checked for authenticity and every crystal for clarity before it reaches your hands — nothing leaves our workbench unverified.

Custom Craftsmanship

Mukhi, size, bead count, thread colour, metal finish — tell us what your practice calls for, and we'll knot it to order.

Worldwide Service

From Patiala to your doorstep, wherever that may be — we pack and ship every order with the same care it was made.
